About CancerMamo

CancerMamo is an innovative health technology project dedicated to improving early breast cancer detection with the power of Artificial Intelligence (AI).

Our platform uses advanced deep learning models to analyze mammogram images and provide accurate, easy-to-understand results that support both healthcare providers and patients.
